About Tycoonist

Designed for long-term progression, not disposable sessions.

Tycoonist focuses on strategy and idle management games that reward planning, return play, and transparent systems. The studio approach emphasizes strong UX, device-friendly performance, and store-ready compliance for privacy, monetization, and player communication.

We believe public trust pages should be simple to find and easy to read. That is why the company site and the game policy are published on a public GitHub Pages URL rather than hidden behind app-only menus or PDF attachments.
